About Us

CHARGE Edu-Tech is a leading organization in the field of education and technology, dedicated to providing quality education and career guidance to students and professionals worldwide. We believe that education is the key to success, and we strive to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to achieve their goals.

Our 4 Week Virtual Internship Program is designed to provide students and professionals with industrial experience and knowledge in their respective domains. Through our program, participants will gain valuable skills in resume building and LinkedIn account creation, which are essential for career development in today's job market.

At CHARGE Edu-Tech, we are committed to providing our interns with the best possible experience. As part of our program, participants will receive an offer letter from CHARGE Edu-Tech, an internship certificate, resume building support, placement support, and a letter of recommendation based on their performance.

We value the information provided by our participants and ensure that it is used for their offer letter and completion certificate. Our team is dedicated to providing support and guidance to our participants throughout their internship program.

Join us on Telegram, LinkedIn, Instagram, and WhatsApp to stay updated on our latest events and offerings. At CHARGE Edu-Tech, we are passionate about education and technology and look forward to helping you achieve your career goals.
